Hawaiian Haystacks

A quick sauce with fix-ins of your choice over health rice.

Sauce

  • 1 11.5oz can Cream of Chicken
  • 1 cup Chicken Broth
  • 1/2 cup Sour Cream
  • 1 12oz can Shredded Chicken

Rice

  • 1 cup White Rice
  • 1.5 cups Water
  • 1 tbs Oil

Optional Toppings

  • Olives
  • Coconut
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Diced Pinnapple
  • Diced Tomatoes

Combine all sauce ingredients in a pot and heat until warm. Combine Rice and water and bring to a boil, cover then simmer for 20 minutes. Pour sauce over rice and add toppings.

Chicken Stir-fry

Chop up some chicken, add a bag of frozen veggies, and combine with this stir fry sauce for a quick delicios meal.

Stir Fry

  • 1 16oz bag Frozen Veggies
  • 1 frozen Chopped Chicken Breast
  • 1 tbs oil

Sauce

  • 1/8 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 2 tbs Ketchup
  • 1 tbs Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Minced Garlic

Rice

  • 1 cup White Rice
  • 1.5 cups Water
  • 1 tbs Oil

Cook frozen veggies and chopped chicken breast in oil in a large skillet. When veggies are soft and chicken is throughouly cooked add sauce and boil for 5 minutes. Serve stirfry over white rice.

Baking Mix Chicken

This quick recipe for chicken fingers pairs well with veggies, rice or potatoes.

Chicken

  • 1 Chicken Breast cut in strips
  • 1 Beaten Egg

Dry mix

  • 2/3 cup Baking Mix
  • 1/2 cup Parmesean Cheese
  • 1 tsp Garlic Salt

Combine the baking mix, parmesean cheese and garlic salt in a bag. Chop chicken breast into strips, dip into beaten egg and place in bag with baking mix. Shake until coated. Place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Back at 450 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.
